О сервисе

Introducing a reliable, secure and fully licensed digital assets platform, where you can easily and conveniently invest in digital securities, buy or sell Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin and Tether.

OKONTO changes the existing approach in the management of the digital assets, providing new opportunities for capital liquidity and access to new financial markets.

We believe in the revolutionary potential of digital asset technology. New technologies eliminate trade barriers, increase the efficiency of financial transactions and radically transform the global financial system.

OKONTO is registered in Estonia and has been carrying out financial operations since 2011. The company’s activity is regulated by the Estonian Financial Intelligence Unit under the provisions of Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing Prevention Act. The company is licensed to buy and sell cryprocurrencies and carrying out financial operations, licensed to provide a custody wallet. We have established relationships with financial institutions, providing us with payment card acquiring, and leading cryptocurrency exchange platforms acting as the liquidity providers for our conversion service.

We strive to make our service convenient and accessible for everyone observing the highest world standards of safety and regulation. Join us!
